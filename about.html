Ileke.Shop is an one stop shop for all your beaded jewelry. Ileke means beads in Yoruba (Nigerian Tribe).
Ileke is used mostly by Nigerian women to adorn their bodies. Ileke.shop is bringing african culture infused with pop culture to create elegant master pieces to beautify every woman.
